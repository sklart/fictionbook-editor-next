ImportEPUB PVS-Studio notes
===========================

fix65 addresses diagnostics from ImportEPUB(3).plog that belong to our own source files:

1. ImportOptionsDialog.cpp, V560
   The lower-bound check for SvgConversionMode compared an unsigned DWORD against zero.
   The condition was simplified to check only the upper bound.

2. EpubImport.cpp, V1048
   CleanTypographyText() contained a no-op assignment for U+2013 EN DASH.
   The redundant assignment was removed.

The remaining diagnostics in ImportEPUB(3).plog belong to bundled third-party LunaSVG/PlutoVG/STB sources under thirdparty\lunasvg.
They are not modified in fix65 to avoid diverging from the upstream library.
For final release it is better to either:
- keep third-party diagnostics as a separate PVS project/baseline, or
- exclude thirdparty\lunasvg from the main ImportEPUB PVS report and track it independently.
